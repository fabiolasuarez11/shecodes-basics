Italian Weeding Soup

One of the best soups from Italian cousine

This Italian Weeding Soup is a perfect mixture of delicious flavors to enjoy during the fall, winter or even summer. My favorite recipe for preparing this flavorful soup is the one from Natasha's Kitchen. The recipe is not just easy to prepare, but has no artificial flavors or processed ingredients, all ingredients are natural and fresh.

The major source of flavor in this soup are little juicy meatballs seasoned with garlic and parmesan cheese. However, the simplicity of this recipe allow you to add your own flavors. I like to add some extra flavor to the ground beef and pork for the meatballs with fresh chives, oregano, parsley and sage. I have known also of people who use veal instead of pork. I think this recipe is a five stars for its simplicity and delicious flavor that remind us that Italian food is beyond of time.
